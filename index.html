1. Introduction

QualitySoft BD is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app রমজানের সময়সূচী ২০২৬ handles information.

Important: The app is completely offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from you.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data, including:

  • Name, email, phone number or any contact details
  • Device ID, IP address, advertising ID
  • Location (GPS or network-based)
  • Photos, contacts, files, microphone or camera data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports

The app only saves a few non-personal preferences locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences:

  • Your chosen district (e.g. Sylhet) to show correct time adjustments
  • Last shown hadith date and ID (so a new hadith appears each day)
  • Which hadiths have already been displayed (to avoid repetition)

This data never leaves your phone and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

3. Permissions

The app requests only one permission:

  • SET_ALARM — to let you set reminders for sehri, iftar or prayer times using your phone’s built-in alarm clock.

No other permissions are used.

4. How We Use Information

Since we collect no personal data, we do not use information for advertising, marketing, profiling or any third-party sharing.

The local preferences are used only to:

  • Display accurate prayer, sehri and iftar times for your selected district
  • Show a different authentic hadith every day
  • Remember your settings between app opens

5. Sharing of Information

We do not share, sell or disclose any information because:

  • No personal data is ever collected
  • No analytics, advertising or tracking libraries are included
  • No third-party services are connected

6. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since no personal data is collected at all, it complies with COPPA and similar laws.

7. Security

All data stays on your device in private Android storage. We follow standard secure development practices. However, absolute security depends also on your device’s protection.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The new version will be posted here with an updated effective date. Major changes (if any) will be shown inside the app.
